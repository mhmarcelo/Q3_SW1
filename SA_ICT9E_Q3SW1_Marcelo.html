Twenty-Five Twenty-One



Native Title: 스물다섯 스물하나
Also Known As: Twenty Five Twenty One, Seumuldaseos Seumulhana, Seumuldaseot Seumulhana, 2521


Synopsis

Twenty Five Twenty One — which shares its title with a 2013 song by acclaimed K-indie rock band Jaurim that is often played in the series — follows five friends who strive to pursue their dreams and navigate early adulthood in the bleak aftermath of the 1997 Asian financial crisis (known as the “IMF Crisis” in South Korea). Despite the devastating recession, they still manage to overcome adversity and experience the joys of adolescence, while picking up some important lessons about love, friendship and personal growth along the way.


Characters


Na Hee Do

The main character who plays the role of a renowned, young, talented, and inspiring fencer holds the name of Na Hee Do, portrayed by the iconic actress Kim Tae-ri.

Baek Yi Jin

Baek Yi JIn, the very passionate newscaster and reporter who always strived and gave his best despite the many hurdles he had to go through, played by the well-known actor Nam Joo-hyuk.

Ko Yu Rim

Another talented fencer who excelled and flourished in her career at a young age, Ko Yu Rim. She was idolized by many for her brilliance, just like the actress who plays her role, Bona.

Mun Ji Ung

The unexpected show stopper, Mun Ji Ung, with his hilarious and quirky personality got the viewers hooked and swooned. Successfully portrayed by Choi Hyun-wook who gained recognition with this role .
